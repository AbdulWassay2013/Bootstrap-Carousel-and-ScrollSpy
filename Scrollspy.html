BMW

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke AG), known as the BMW Group, is a German multinational manufacturer of luxury vehicles and motorcycles, headquartered in Munich, Bavaria, Germany. The company's history traces back to 1916, initially producing aircraft engines. Today, BMW operates under several brands including BMW, MINI, Rolls-Royce, and BMW Motorrad.
